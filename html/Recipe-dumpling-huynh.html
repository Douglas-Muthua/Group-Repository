My Dumpling recipe

This recipe is adapted from my family recipe.

Ingredient

  • 1 bag dumpling wrapper
  • 1 pound of pork
  • 1 cup of chopped carrot
  • 1 tbsp of chopped garlic
  • 1 tbsp of chopped shallot
  • 2 green onion
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp of fish sauce

Equipment

  • big bowl
  • tablespoon
  • pot
  • knife
  • cutting board

Direction

step 1. Put 1 pound of ground pork in a big bowl then use the knife to cut 2 green onion in a cutting board and put everything except for the dumpling wrapper inside the big bowl.

step 2. Mixed everything together and seasoning the mixture according to your taste.

step 3. take the dumpling wrapper out of the plastic bag, place a piece of the dumpling wrapper in your palm.

step 4. use a tablespoon to scoop the meat mixture and place the appropriate amount upon the dumpling wrappers.

step 5. slightly cup your palm around dumpling and gently grasp edge of wrapper between your thumb and index finger. carefully fold stretched area on itself creating a pleat.pinch to seal.

step 6. put a pot to the stove pour in 3 cup of water let the water hot boil then put your dumpling in and boiled for 10-15 minutes.

you can also fried or steamed your dumpling.
