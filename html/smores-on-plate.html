S'MORES

This recipe is created by Emre Selcuk himself. He is a world renown connoisseur of s'mores within the food industry.

Ingredients

  • 1 Hershey Bar
  • 1 Box Of Graham Crackers
  • 1 Bag of Medium Sized Marshmellows

Directions

  1. Put the firewood into the campfire and put the fire starters against the wood. Light the fire starts and ensure that they are going to catch the wood on fire. Safely tend to the campfire until it can sustain itself.
  2. Remove all ingredients from their packing. Grab a medium sized marshmallow and attach it to the end of the stick. Slowly and carefully melt the marshmallow until golden brown.
  3. Once the marshmallow is golden brown, break a graham cracker in half. Break 4 pieces of the Hershey bar off and place it onto one half of the graham cracker.
  4. Place the cooked marshmallow on the graham cracker that has the chocolate on it, then use the other half of the graham cracker to smush the marshmallow into the chocolate.
